# Economy

## Core Loop

Collect Junk -> Bring Junk to Grunk -> Receive Grunk -> Spend Grunk with Shellvis

## Junk

Junk is gathered throughout worlds. It can be debris, forgotten objects, odd treasures, local nonsense, or anything that looks like it might have emotional value to a goblin appraiser.

## Grunk The Character

Species: Unknown

Role: Currency Exchange NPC

Players sell junk to Grunk.

Grunk pays players in Grunk.

Grunk only says the word "Grunk."

Nobody comments on this.

## Grunk The Currency

The game's currency is also called Grunk.

Grunk is the main economy material for expressive rewards, hub decoration, and other non-power progression.

Players receive Grunk from Grunk after selling junk.

## Shellvis Shop

Shellvis sells:
- Shell cosmetics
- Slingshot cosmetics
- Dash effects
- Hub decorations
- Special nonsense offers

Shellvis accepts Grunk. Shellvis does not appraise junk.

## Anchor Polish

Shellvis sells Anchor Polish.

Anchor Polish is used to clean and restore Beam Anchors.

Purpose:

- Gives Grunk another practical use
- Connects Shellvis to world restoration
- Makes checkpoints feel physical and diegetic
- Supports the "Clean All The Anchors" achievement
- Reinforces progression through restoration and expression, not power

Example item:

```txt
Anchor Polish
Cost: 25 Grunk
Description: Makes anchor less embarrassing.
```

Possible Shellvis dialogue:

```txt
"Anchor look rough."

"Need polish."

"Conveniently, I sell polish."

"What's in it?"

"Polish."

"Rude."
```

Late-game joke item:

```txt
Premium Anchor Polish
Cost: 500 Grunk
Description: Mostly polish.
```

## Cosmetics

Purpose:
- Express personality
- Reinforce humor
- Reward exploration
- Deepen world identity

Cosmetics should:
- Never increase power
- Create conversation
- Reinforce tone

Examples:
- Shell variants
- Slingshot variants
- Dash effects
- Hub decorations

Special cosmetics may include unique dialogue reactions.

Example:
- Emperor's New Shell: NPCs react positively despite no visible change.

## Bobbleheads

Bobbleheads are a candidate collectible category.

Possible roles:
- World completion reward
- Hidden curiosity reward
- Hub display item
- Collector NPC interaction

## Principles

- Grunk should feel useful without becoming mandatory homework.
- Rewards should be charming, funny, expressive, or mysterious.
- Avoid combat stat upgrades and power scaling.
- Economy rewards should support identity and exploration, not strength.
