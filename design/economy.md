# Economy

## Core Currency

The player collects junk and converts it into Grunk.

Grunk is the main economy material for expressive rewards, hub decoration, and other non-power progression.

## Spend Targets

- Cosmetics
- Shell skins
- Dash effects
- Slingshot variants
- Hub decorations
- Collectible displays
- Funny merchant offers
- World-specific oddities

## Principles

- Grunk should feel useful without becoming mandatory homework.
- Rewards should be charming, funny, expressive, or mysterious.
- Avoid combat stat upgrades and power scaling.

## Open Questions

- Does all junk convert into Grunk at the same rate?
- Are some junk pieces unique collectibles?
- Does Shellvis control the whole economy, or only a suspicious slice of it?
