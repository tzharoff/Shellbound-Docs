# Player

## Main Character Overview

The player controls a small turtle who leaves home after a mysterious disappearance and begins restoring ancient beams that reconnect distant worlds.

The player is not chosen, powerful, or special. They are simply willing to continue.

The player never speaks.

## Role

- Player
- Explorer
- Collector
- Beam restorer
- Quiet witness
