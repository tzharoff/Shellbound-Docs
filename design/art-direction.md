# Art Direction

## PS1 Style Guide

Shellbound should feel inspired by PS1 and early 64-bit adventure games.

Target qualities:
- Low-poly forms
- Chunky silhouettes
- Clear colors
- Readable spaces
- Simple materials
- Slightly strange texture choices
- Nostalgic without becoming unreadable

## Environment Rules

Worlds should be:
- Colorful and memorable
- Easy to describe
- Designed around movement routes
- Full of landmarks
- Hiding optional weirdness at the edges

## Character Style

Characters should have:
- Strong silhouettes
- Simple readable shapes
- Expressive animation
- A little handmade awkwardness

Shellvis costume notes:
- Fake shell
- Visible straps
- Slight shell shifting
- Goblin features occasionally visible

## Animation Style

Animation should prioritize readability and charm.

Examples:
- Expressive turtle movement
- Simple portrait flaps
- Low-frame charm
- Squash and stretch where useful
- Bosses with slow ancient weight
