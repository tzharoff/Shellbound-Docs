# Core Loop

## Primary Loop

1. Enter a world.
2. Move through inviting spaces.
3. Collect junk, secrets, and oddities.
4. Convert junk into Grunk.
5. Spend Grunk on expressive rewards or useful access.
6. Restore ancient beams.
7. Open new routes, reactions, and mysteries.

## Reward Types

- Cosmetics
- Shell skins
- Dash effects
- Slingshot variants
- Hub decorations
- Collectibles
- Dialogue reactions
- Lore fragments
- New understanding of the world

## Notes

The loop should stay simple enough to explain quickly, but flexible enough to support strange local rules in each world.
