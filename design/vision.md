# Vision

Shellbound is a third-person adventure collectathon starring a small turtle in a world that feels cheerful on the surface and ancient underneath.

The game should feel like a half-remembered PS1 or early 64-bit adventure: chunky shapes, bold silhouettes, readable spaces, playful secrets, and just enough unease to make the world feel older than it looks.

## High Concept

Explore strange connected worlds, collect junk, convert it into Grunk, restore ancient beams, and slowly uncover what the island has been trying to say.

## Experience Goals

- Movement should feel good before anything else is layered on top.
- Exploration should reward curiosity, not checklist exhaustion.
- Weird lore should be discoverable, optional, and delightful to chase.
- Progress should make the player more expressive, not more powerful.
- The world should feel cozy, suspicious, funny, and slightly haunted.

## Current Status

Early concept / pre-prototype.
