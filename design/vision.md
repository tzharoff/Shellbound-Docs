# Vision

Shellbound is a third-person adventure collectathon inspired by the 32-bit and early 64-bit era of video games.

Players control a small turtle who leaves home after a mysterious disappearance and discovers that distant worlds are no longer connected like they once were. By restoring forgotten beam pathways, the turtle travels across beaches, forests, forgotten places, and impossible dreamlike lands.

Most players should experience Shellbound as a cozy retro adventure. Players who look closer may discover something stranger.

## High Concept

Explore strange connected worlds, gather valuable junk, bring that junk to Grunk, spend Grunk with Shellvis, restore ancient beams, and search for the Great Turtle.

## Tone

- 80% cozy adventure
- 15% strange humor
- 5% "Wait... what did that mean?"

## Player Experience Goals

### Feel Joy In Movement

Players should enjoy moving through the world even without objectives.

Desired reactions:
- "Jumping feels good."
- "I want to explore."
- "I found shortcuts."

### Feel Wonder And Discovery

Players should feel curious about what is around the next corner.

Desired reactions:
- "What is that?"
- "I wonder where this goes."
- "That world looks cool."

### Feel Rewarded For Curiosity

Players who explore should discover meaningful or funny content.

Desired reactions:
- "Glad I checked."
- "Wait... that's weird."
- "I found something."

### Feel Comfortable And Nostalgic

The game should feel inspired by late 90s and early 2000s adventures without becoming a strict imitation.

Examples:
- Low-poly visuals
- Simple systems
- Colorful worlds
- Chunky, readable spaces

### Feel Small In A Large World

The player is not a chosen hero. The world existed before them and will continue after them.

Examples:
- Ancient beams
- Giant landmarks
- Strange NPCs
- Places that feel older than they should

### Feel Curious, Not Confused

Immediate goals should be clear. Deeper meaning should feel optional.

Good reaction:
- "That was weird."

Bad reaction:
- "I do not understand what is happening."

### Feel Progress Without Power

The turtle should feel more personal over time, not stronger through stats.

Examples:
- Shell skins
- Dash effects
- Hub decoration
- Collectibles
- Dialogue reactions
