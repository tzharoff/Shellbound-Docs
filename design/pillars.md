# Pillars

## Movement First

Running, jumping, turning, camera control, and Shell Dash should feel satisfying in simple spaces before the game asks the player to do anything complicated.

## Collectathon Joy

The player collects junk, treasures, oddities, secrets, and world-specific nonsense. Collection should feel playful and tactile.

## Strange Connected Worlds

Worlds should feel distinct but linked by deeper rules, recurring symbols, and ancient beam technology.

## Expression Over Power

Progression should focus on cosmetics, shell skins, dash effects, slingshot variants, hub decoration, dialogue reactions, and world understanding.

## Optional Weirdness

Players can enjoy the surface adventure without decoding everything. Curious players should find stranger truths underneath.
