# Risks

## Movement Feel

If movement is not fun early, the collectathon loop will not carry the game.

## Reward Value

Expression-based progression needs enough charm and variety to replace traditional power upgrades.

## Weirdness Balance

The deeper lore should reward curious players without making casual players feel locked out of the core story.

## Scope

Connected worlds, collectibles, cosmetics, lore, and characters can grow quickly. Keep the first playable slice small.
