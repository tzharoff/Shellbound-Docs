# Risks

## Movement Feel

If movement is not fun early, the collectathon loop will not carry the game.

Mitigation:
- Prototype movement first.
- Test in empty spaces before adding content.
- Tune camera and Shell Dash before building complex levels.

## Reward Value

Expression-based progression needs enough charm and variety to replace traditional power upgrades.

Mitigation:
- Prototype cosmetics early.
- Add NPC reactions to some rewards.
- Make hub decoration visible and satisfying.

## Weirdness Balance

The deeper lore should reward curious players without making casual players feel locked out of the core story.

Mitigation:
- Keep immediate goals simple.
- Put strange details in optional dialogue, secrets, and repeated patterns.
- Avoid mandatory lore decoding.

## Scope

Connected worlds, collectibles, cosmetics, lore, and characters can grow quickly. Keep the first playable slice small.

Mitigation:
- Build one complete loop before expanding world count.
- Treat the vertical slice as proof, not content production.

## Canon Drift

Shellbound has playful names and overlapping concepts, especially Grunk the character and Grunk the currency.

Mitigation:
- Keep glossary updated.
- Record canon-changing decisions.
- Update markdown sections when the GDD changes.
