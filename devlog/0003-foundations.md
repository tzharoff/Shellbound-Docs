# Devlog 0003 - Foundations

## Date

2026-05-28

## Summary

Established the initial foundation for Shellbound as a cozy, strange, PS1-inspired turtle collectathon.

## Accomplished

- Defined the high concept.
- Established the core pillars.
- Outlined the player experience goals.
- Created the initial core loop.
- Defined the first major characters.
- Established the three-act world structure.
- Clarified that progression is based on expression, not power.

## Key Decisions

- Shellbound should be movement-first.
- The game should reward curiosity.
- Weirdness should be optional, not required to understand the game.
- Player progression should not use stat upgrades.
- Cosmetics, collectibles, hub decoration, and understanding are the primary rewards.
