# Devlog 0003 - Shellvis and Grunk Everywhere

## Date

2026-06-02

## Summary

Established Shellvis and Grunk as recurring constants throughout Shellbound.

Both characters now appear in every world as part of the game's economy and ongoing weirdness.

## Accomplished

- Decided Shellvis appears in every world.
- Decided Grunk appears in every world.
- Removed the requirement to return to the Hub World for economy interactions.
- Established that neither character's travel methods will be explained.
- Connected their presence to the Optional Weirdness pillar.
- Expanded the concept of Grunk beyond simply being a goblin or a currency.

## Key Decisions

- Shellvis operates a shop in every world.
- Grunk exchanges junk for Grunk in every world.
- Their appearances are treated as normal by most NPCs.
- The game never provides a definitive explanation.

## Character Development

Grunk is now intentionally ambiguous.

The player may encounter NPCs who believe Grunk is:

- a goblin
- a currency
- a religion
- a market force
- all of the above

No interpretation is confirmed.

## Shellvis World Variants

Shellvis should wear world-themed cosmetics that showcase rewards available in the current world.

Examples:

Sunny Shore:
- Sailor cap

Endless Tracks:
- Conductor outfit

Forgotten Aquarium:
- Diving suit

Sugarwind Highlands:
- Candy merchant attire

This allows Shellvis to subtly preview cosmetic rewards while reinforcing world identity.
