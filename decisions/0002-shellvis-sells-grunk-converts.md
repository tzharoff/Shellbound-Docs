# DDR-0002 - Shellvis Sells, Grunk Converts

## Status

Accepted

## Decision

Shellvis is the cosmetic merchant. Shellvis sells expressive rewards and accepts Grunk.

Grunk is the junk appraiser. Grunk evaluates junk and converts it into Grunk.

This means the economy loop is:

1. Collect junk.
2. Bring junk to Grunk.
3. Receive Grunk.
4. Spend Grunk with Shellvis.

## Reason

Splitting these roles makes the hub funnier, clearer, and more characterful.

Shellvis can focus on suspicious salesmanship and cosmetic rewards. Grunk can own the junk appraisal loop and the running joke that everyone understands "Grunk" except the player.

## Alternatives Considered

- Shellvis appraises junk and sells cosmetics.
- Grunk is only the currency name.
- Junk converts automatically without a character interaction.

## Consequences

- The hub needs both a Shellvis shop and a Grunk stall.
- Dialogue and UI should distinguish junk appraisal from cosmetic shopping.
- The glossary must be careful because Grunk is both a character and a currency.
- Old notes where Shellvis converts junk should be treated as cut canon.
