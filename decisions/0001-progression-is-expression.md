# DDR-0001 - Progression Is Expression

## Status

Accepted

## Decision

Shellbound will not use traditional stat-based power progression.

The player should not gain:
- Damage upgrades
- Health upgrades as the main progression path
- Level-based power
- Gear with combat stats

Instead, progression should focus on:
- Cosmetics
- Shell skins
- Dash effects
- Slingshot variants
- Hub decorations
- Collectibles
- Dialogue reactions
- New understanding of the world

## Reason

Shellbound is about curiosity, movement, identity, and discovery.

The player should feel more connected to the world and more expressive over time, but earlier content should not become invalidated by power scaling.

## Alternatives Considered

- RPG leveling
- Damage upgrades
- Ability trees
- Gear-based progression

## Consequences

- Rewards must be charming, funny, cosmetic, or discovery-based.
- The economy needs enough expressive unlocks to feel worthwhile.
- Exploration rewards need strong personality.
