# DDR-0003 - Shellvis and Grunk Exist Everywhere

## Status

Accepted

## Decision

Shellvis and Grunk appear in:

- The Great Shell (Hub World)
- Sunny Shore
- Endless Tracks
- Beam Forest
- Forgotten Aquarium
- Sugarwind Highlands
- Skyward Shell
- Turtle Temple
- Turtles All The Way Down

The player can always:

- Sell junk to Grunk
- Receive Grunk currency
- Spend Grunk with Shellvis

without needing to return to the Hub World.

## Reason

The economy should remain accessible without interrupting exploration.

More importantly, Shellvis and Grunk are recurring characters and part of the world's strange consistency.

The player should gradually realize:

Shellvis is always here.

Grunk is always here.

Nobody seems surprised.

## Narrative Rule

Do not explain how they travel.

Do not create transportation lore.

Do not create teleportation lore.

Do not provide a definitive answer.

The mystery is more entertaining than the explanation.

## Optional Weirdness

Most players will simply accept:

"That's the shop."

Players who think deeper may ask:

- How does Shellvis get here?
- How does Grunk get here?
- Did they travel?
- Are there multiple Grunks?
- Is there only one Shellvis?
- Have they always been here?

No canonical answer should be provided.

## Consequences

- Every world requires a Shellvis location.
- Every world requires a Grunk location.
- Their appearance should reinforce world identity.
- Their presence should contribute to the game's recurring weirdness.
