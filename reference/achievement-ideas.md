# Achievement Ideas

## Clean All The Anchors

Restore every Beam Anchor.

Reward:

- Old Sailor's Hat

Notes:

This achievement should feel wholesome, practical, and slightly silly.

The player is not rewarded with power. They are rewarded with expression.

## Still Connected

Restore your first Beam Anchor.

## Leave The Beam On

Complete the main adventure.

## Pleasure Doin' Shellness

Purchase your first item from Shellvis.

## Red Remember

Defeat the Great Red Lobstrocity.

## You Look Familiar

Meet The Kid in multiple worlds.

## Time Is Funny Here

Encounter an impossible memory.

## Another Shell Below

Find a major hidden secret.
