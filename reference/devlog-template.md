# Devlog Template

Use this template when a devlog entry is meant to capture a decision, validation pass, prototype result, or design lesson.

The format can be adjusted for the entry, but the goal should stay the same: explain the decision, show how it was tested, and record what changed because of it.

```md
# Devlog 0000 - Entry Title

## Date

YYYY-MM-DD

## Summary

A short explanation of what this entry covers and why it matters.

## Decision

What was decided, changed, tested, or questioned?

## Why

What problem is this meant to solve?

What assumption are we making?

## Validation

How did we test or review the decision?

Examples:

- Prototype
- Playtest
- Design pillar review
- Scope review
- Technical feasibility check
- Content review

## Result

What happened?

Did the decision hold up, partially hold up, or fail?

## Follow-Up

What changes, tasks, or questions come next?

## Lesson

What should another developer learn from this?
```

## Lightweight Version

For smaller entries, use this shorter structure:

```md
# Devlog 0000 - Entry Title

## Date

YYYY-MM-DD

## Decision

What are we choosing?

## Validation

How did we check whether that choice makes sense?

## Outcome

What did we learn, and what happens next?
```
