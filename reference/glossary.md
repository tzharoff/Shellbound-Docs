# Glossary

## Grunk

Meaning:
Unknown.

May refer to:

- Grunk (the goblin)
- Grunk (the currency)
- Grunk (the concept)
- Grunk (the institution)
- Grunk (the religion)

No canonical explanation exists.

This is intentional.

## Shellvis

Merchant.

Wears an obviously fake turtle costume.

Operates shops in every world.

Nobody comments on this.

This is also intentional.

## The Great Shell

The hub world. It contains the Shellvis shop, Grunk stall, beam platform, decoration areas, and traveler space.

## Ancient Beams

Mysterious structures or connections the player restores to progress through the world.

## Shell Dash

A core movement action for fast traversal, impact, and turtle confidence.

## Junk

Collectable debris, treasures, and oddities found throughout the worlds.

## Expression Progression

Progression through cosmetics, decoration, reactions, collections, and understanding instead of stat-based power.

## Emperor's New Shell

A cosmetic for the discerning turtle. NPCs react positively. The player sees no visible change. No explanation provided.

## Shellbert

A recurring symbol-speaking turtle who appears in strange places.

## The Kid

A recurring child turtle who anchors emotion and time weirdness.

## The Great Turtle

The ancient being tied to the shell network. Not god. Not ruler. Just impossibly old.
